Hobbies

Sport

I have been playing and watching football since childhood. This sport has always been more than a hobby for me — I have been a devoted fan of it since an early age.

Music

Music has always held a special place in my life. I like to listen to compositions of different genres and artists, finding something unique and inspiring in each piece. Music helps to relax, energizes and opens up new horizons of creativity.

Movie

I love watching movies and TV series, because it's an opportunity to immerse yourself in different worlds and experience exciting stories. One of my most favorite series is Game of Thrones — for its incredible atmosphere, multi-layered plot and unforgettable characters.

Mountains

I love spending time with my friends outdoors, walking through the mountains, enjoying their majestic nature and tranquility. Walking along mountain trails fills me with energy and gives me the opportunity to escape from the daily hustle and bustle.
